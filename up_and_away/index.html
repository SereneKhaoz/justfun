About

I'm passionate about comics. I am a fan of the major publishers such as DC, Image, and Marvel as well as a fan of independant comc book publishers. I watch the movies, animations, and TV shows. This is my personal site is where I share some of my thoughts on comics, music, and technology.

This summer I've taken two classes from the Smithsonian on comic books and pop culture. The first class, POPx1.4 Rise of the Superheroes. The second course is POPX2.1 Rise of the Superoes and the Heroes of the Future. I had to create characters for each of the courses. The characters on this page are the ones I created.
